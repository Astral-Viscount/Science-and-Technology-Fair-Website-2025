For Judges

Thank you for volunteering. This page summarises the judging process, expected conduct, and scoring guidance.

Before the fair

  • Read the judging rubric and category guidelines in Resources.
  • Attend the judges' briefing (time will be provided with your assignment).
  • Declare any conflicts of interest and request a reassignment if needed.

During judging

  • Introduce yourself to students and be encouraging — the interview is part of the assessment.
  • Use the score sheet to record objective ratings and brief feedback.
  • Focus on the rubric: question, method, data quality, interpretation, and communication.

Scoring & feedback

Scores should be evidence-based. Provide constructive comments that students and teachers can use to improve future work.
