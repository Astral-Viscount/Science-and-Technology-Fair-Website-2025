For Teachers

Resources and advice for supervising projects, coordinating entries, and supporting students through the process.

Supervising projects

Teachers should ensure projects meet safety and ethical requirements, sign off on high-risk activities, and keep records of approvals.

Supervising Projects guidance

Entry process

Schools are responsible for collating entries and submitting them by the published deadline. Keep an eye on the Events page for exact dates.

Assessment support

Encourage students to practice their interviews and prepare concise summaries. Use the judging rubric to highlight assessment expectations.
