Supervising projects

Teachers play a key role in ensuring student safety, ethical compliance, and good research practice.

Checklist for supervisors

  • Review project plans early and sign off any that involve hazards or human/animal participants.
  • Ensure students keep an up-to-date logbook with dates, procedures and results.
  • Confirm any fieldwork has appropriate permissions and risk assessments.
  • Advise on data handling, consent forms, and privacy for human participants.

Approvals

Use the Ethics & Safety checklist in Resources. For work requiring institutional approval, allow extra time before the fair.

Supporting presentations

Help students distil findings into a clear poster or board. Encourage practice interviews and peer review sessions.
