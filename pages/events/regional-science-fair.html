Regional Science Fair

Date: 15 September 2025
Time: 9:00–17:00
Location: Christchurch Town Hall

A competition showcasing student STEM projects from across the region. Projects are exhibited throughout the day; judging occurs in the morning; the public viewing is in the afternoon.

Schedule

  • 09:00 – Exhibitor setup
  • 10:00 – Judging begins
  • 13:00 – Public viewing
  • 17:00 – Pack down
