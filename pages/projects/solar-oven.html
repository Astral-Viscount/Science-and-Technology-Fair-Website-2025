Build a Solar Oven

By Jane Doe — Year 11 • Category: Environment, Engineering

This project explores how reflectors and insulation affect a simple solar cooker’s ability to reach safe cooking temperatures. Multiple designs were tested with data logged at 1-minute intervals.

Results

  • Peak internal temperature: 92°C on a 19°C day
  • Best reflector angle: 45° at solar noon
  • Insulation upgrade (corrugated cardboard + wool) improved heat retention by ~18%
