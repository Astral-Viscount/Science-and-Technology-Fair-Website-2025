Prizes

Recognising excellent science, innovation, and communication across categories and year levels. Prize types include sponsored awards, category winners, and special achievement prizes.

Major prize categories

  • Science Investigation (Years 7–13)
  • Technology & Engineering (Prototype & Design)
  • Environmental & Agricultural Science
  • Health & Human Sciences
  • Junior (Years 7–10) and Senior (Years 11–13) awards

Sponsored awards

Local organisations sponsor targeted prizes (e.g., sustainability, innovation, STEM outreach). Sponsored awards are judged by representatives from the sponsor alongside the chief judge.

Past winners

How prizes are decided

  1. Judges assess projects against the published rubric (clarity of question, method, analysis, presentation, and originality).
  2. Category finalists are selected during judging rounds; winners are chosen by consensus or panel scoring.
  3. Special awards may consider additional criteria (e.g., commercial potential, community impact).

See the Resources page for the judging rubric and guidance for entrants.
